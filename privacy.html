Numby — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 3, 2026

1. The short version

Numby works entirely on your device. We run no servers and hold no user accounts. Your artwork, coloring progress, and settings never leave your phone. The only data collection happens through two Google services: AdMob (ads for free users) and Google Play Billing (purchases).

2. Data stored on your device only

  • Coloring progress and generated artworks — saved in the app’s local storage.
  • Settings and subscription status — a local flag; ownership itself is verified with Google Play.

We never see this data. It is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising (free tier)

Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect your resettable advertising ID and standard device information to serve and measure ads. In the European Economic Area and the UK you will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, and you can change that choice at any time. You can also reset or delete your advertising ID in your device’s Google settings. Details: policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Premium removes all ads.

4. Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed entirely by Google Play. We never receive your name, address, or payment details. The app only learns whether your Google account owns a product, so it can unlock premium and restore purchases after a reinstall.

5. Children

Numby is directed at users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app and data was collected via the ad SDK, contact us and we will help address it.

6. Data retention & deletion

Because everything is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently deletes your artwork, progress, and settings. Data collected by Google (advertising, purchase history) is governed by Google’s own policies and controls at myaccount.google.com.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. For the on-device data described above, you are in direct control. For anything else, email us and we will help within 30 days.

8. Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier).
  • Google Play Billing — purchases and subscriptions.

No other third party receives data from this app.

9. Changes

We will update the “Effective” date above when this policy changes. Material changes will be highlighted in the app.
